Sarvah Crafts is a socially-conscious enterprise, proudly led by women, dedicated to uplifting marginalized artisan communities throughout India by providing them with marketing linkages for their products. Our mission is to revolutionize the sales of products made by NGOS, Self help groups and direct artisans, thereby fostering economic independence for them.
